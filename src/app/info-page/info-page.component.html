Stat Crusher



This app was made to gain insight into how players typically build their Pokemon. This is extremely useful for speed creeping or offensive and defensive damage calculations. It is currently only designed with VGC in mind but in the future will accomodate other formats like OU. It was largely inspired by sites like Pikalytics and Babiri.net.
